Taylor C. Johnson, GISP

I am a geographer, cartographer, and geospatial application developer. After earning my BSc and MSc I decided to begin my career as a geographic information specialist in the public sector. Over the past decade, I have worked for various state and federal agencies including the US Army Corps of Engineers and the US Geological Survey most recently. I enjoy crafting tools and cartographic products for effective geospatial analysis and visualization.

For desktop applications, I frequently use the Python arcpy module for geoprocessing applications in ArcGIS. Additionally, I have been reintroducing myself to ESRI's ArcObjects SDK in the .NET framework (using VB.Net and C#). My experience in web-based applications has grown significantly over the past year. In the past, I have used the Google Maps API but am exploring the recent versions of ESRI's Javascript API and mapbox.js (based on Leaflet).
